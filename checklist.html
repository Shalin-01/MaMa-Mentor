Baby Essentials Checklist

Clothing

  • Onesies or bodysuits
  • Sleepers or pajamas
  • Socks and mittens
  • Hats (warm and lightweight)
  • Swaddle blankets

Feeding

  • Bottles and nipples
  • Breast pump
  • Formula (if needed)
  • Burp cloths
  • Bibs
  • Nursing pillow

Diapering

  • Diapers (disposable or cloth)
  • Baby wipes
  • Diaper rash cream
  • Changing pad
  • Diaper bag

Sleeping

  • Crib or bassinet
  • Mattress and fitted sheets
  • Baby monitor
  • Nightlight

Bathing

  • Baby bathtub
  • Baby shampoo and body wash
  • Hooded towels
  • Washcloths

Health & Safety

  • Infant first aid kit
  • Thermometer
  • Nail clippers or file
  • Nasal aspirator

Travel

  • Car seat
  • Stroller
  • Baby carrier or wrap
